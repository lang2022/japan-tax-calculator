How to plan your basket

  1. List your items and note whether each one is a general good or a consumable.
  2. Add the subtotals for each category.
  3. Run each grouped amount in the calculator and compare store types if needed.

Example list

  • Cosmetics: ¥3,200 (consumables)
  • Snacks: ¥2,100 (consumables)
  • Headphones: ¥12,000 (general goods)

In this example, the consumables subtotal is ¥5,300 and the general-goods subtotal is ¥12,000. Checking both subtotals separately helps you understand how category and store policy affect the result.

What to watch for

  • Department store service fees can reduce your final refund.
  • Mixed baskets may be treated differently depending on the store.
  • Threshold and handling rules can vary by store policy.

FAQ

Can I plan mixed categories together?

Yes. Group general goods and consumables separately, then compare the totals in the calculator.

How should I handle service fees?

Run the same list once as a standard shop and once as a department store to see the difference.

What is the easiest way to save my plan?

Keep your item totals in your notes app, then run the final grouped amounts through the calculator.
