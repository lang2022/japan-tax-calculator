General Goods vs Consumables (2026)

Knowing the category helps you avoid calculation mistakes and checkout surprises.

Calculate as general goods Calculate as consumables

What counts as general goods?

Examples: clothing, electronics, souvenirs. Follow the store’s category.

What counts as consumables?

Examples: food, cosmetics, medicine. Some stores may apply special handling or packaging instructions.

Why the category matters

Category can affect the tax rate and how the store processes your tax-free purchase.

FAQ

How do I know if an item is ‘consumable’ or ‘general’?

Follow the store’s category and signage. When unsure, ask staff.

Do consumables have special packaging rules?

Sometimes. Policies vary—check store instructions to avoid issues.

Are the tax rates always the same?

Rates and rules may change. Always follow the store’s current guidance.
