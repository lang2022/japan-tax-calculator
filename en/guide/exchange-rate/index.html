FAQ

Which exchange rate should I use?

Rates can differ by payment method (card vs cash). Use a reasonable estimate.

Will my estimate be exact?

Not always—fees and actual settlement rate can cause differences.

Which date’s rate should I use?

Using the rate around your purchase date usually reduces error.
